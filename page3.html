India's food is revered worldwide for its diverse and flavoral options!

North Indian Food to Try

Chaats: Fried dough topped with sweet, spicy and tangy vegetables

Samosa: A pocket of fried dough stuffered with spicy potatoes and other vegetables

Chole Bature: Spicy white chickpea curry with some fluffy fried bread

Thali: A large plate with multiple small round plates filled with famous chutnies and currys for fried bread

South Indian Food to Try

Dosas: a thin pancake made from fermented batter of various lentils

Idlis: rice cakes made from lentil and rice batter

Biriyani: a spicy mix of rice, meat, and vegetables

Vada: flavored batter that is deep fried in oil

West Indian Food to Try

Fish Curry: fish stew made with a blend of indian spices and vegetables

Vada Pav: a deep fried potatoe dumpling stuffed inside a bun

Zunkha Bhakari: a millet bread with chickpea flour and vegetables

Bombay Duck: a type of fish lathered in spices to make a since crisy snack

East Indian Food to Try

Rasagulla: a milk based sweet made from curdling milk

Dalma: mixed vegetable curry

Rasmalai: sweet Indian cottage cheese

Thukpa: traditional noodle soup consisting of many vegetables and flavorful meat
